Cobra Kai

A continuation of its predecessor Karate Kid, Johnny Lawrence and Daniel LaRusso are back again but this time as adults. Their lives have taken different routes with Daniel as a car company owner and Johnny as a jobless man. When a new kid Miguel comes into his life and asks him to teach him Karate, Johnny agrees and opens up Cobra Kai, a karate dojo, again. How is everyone going to deal with this?
