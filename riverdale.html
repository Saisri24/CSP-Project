Riverdale

A mystery and drama-filled show taking place in a small town nobody knows of called Riverdale, the show begins with Archie Andrews (played by KJ Apa, the protagonist, entering sophomore year of high school with the weight of the world on his shoulders. Veronica Lodge, the new girl, attends Riverdale High recently after the town’s golden boy Jason Blossom was found dead. Drama and mysteries unfold as the main characters discover the truth behind Jason Blossom’s death and explore their relationships with each other.
